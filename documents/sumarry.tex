## Unconstrained NMF
NMF receives input matrix $V_{(FxN)}$ and number of factors $K$.

And it outputs non-negative matrices $W_{FxK}$, $H_{KxN}$: 

$V \approx WH$

Solve:

$\hat{W},\hat{H} = \underset{W\geq0,H\geq0}{\operatorname{argmin}} d_{\beta}(V|WH)$ 


Method
